Common Questions

FAQ — Cancun Private Transfers

Is a private transfer safer than a taxi at CUN?

Significantly. Our Cancun Private Transfers use only vetted, licensed drivers and fully insured vehicles — far more reliable and secure than unregulated street taxis.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 24–48 hours ahead, especially during peak season (December–April). That said, we often accommodate same-day bookings — just reach out via WhatsApp.

What if my flight is delayed or cancelled?

We monitor all flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver waits at no extra charge. For cancellations, we offer full flexibility on rebooking at no penalty.

Can you handle large groups or destination weddings?

Absolutely. Our Sprinter vans and coordinated multi-vehicle transfers are built for large groups. Contact us via WhatsApp for a custom group quote.

Do you offer return transfers back to the airport?

Yes! Book round-trip Cancun Private Transfers at the same time and save. Your return pickup time and driver details are confirmed 24 hours before departure.

Are car seats available for young children?

Yes, child safety seats are available on request at no extra cost. Simply mention the ages of your children at the time of booking and we'll have everything ready.
